# Reading List

## Core References

### Quantum Mechanics & Spin Dynamics

1. **Nielsen & Chuang, "Quantum Computation and Quantum Information"**
   - Chapter 2: Introduction to Quantum Mechanics
   - Chapter 4: Quantum circuits
   - Focus: Spin-1/2 systems, Bloch sphere representation

2. **Sakurai & Napolitano, "Modern Quantum Mechanics"**
   - Chapter 3: Theory of Angular Momentum
   - Focus: Spin precession, Larmor frequency

3. **Griffiths, "Introduction to Quantum Mechanics"**
   - Chapter 4: Quantum Mechanics in Three Dimensions
   - Focus: Spin operators, Pauli matrices

### Quantum Dots & Spin Qubits

4. **Loss & DiVincenzo, "Quantum computation with quantum dots"**
   - Phys. Rev. A 57, 120 (1998)
   - https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevA.57.120
   - **Key**: Original proposal for spin-based quantum computation in quantum dots

5. **Hanson et al., "Spins in few-electron quantum dots"**
   - Rev. Mod. Phys. 79, 1217 (2007)
   - https://doi.org/10.1103/RevModPhys.79.1217
   - **Key**: Comprehensive review of spin qubits in quantum dots

6. **Zwanenburg et al., "Silicon quantum electronics"**
   - Rev. Mod. Phys. 85, 961 (2013)
   - https://doi.org/10.1103/RevModPhys.85.961
   - **Key**: Silicon-based spin qubits, exchange coupling

### Exchange Coupling & Two-Spin Systems

7. **Burkard et al., "Coupled quantum dots as quantum gates"**
   - Phys. Rev. B 59, 2070 (1999)
   - https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevB.59.2070
   - **Key**: Two-spin exchange coupling, SWAP gate

8. **Petta et al., "Coherent manipulation of coupled electron spins in semiconductor quantum dots"**
   - Science 309, 2180 (2005)
   - https://doi.org/10.1126/science.1116955
   - **Key**: Experimental demonstration of two-spin exchange

### Decoherence & Open Systems

9. **Breuer & Petruccione, "The Theory of Open Quantum Systems"**
   - Chapter 3: Quantum Master Equations
   - **Key**: Lindblad master equation, T1/T2 times

10. **Nielsen & Chuang, Chapter 8: Quantum Noise and Quantum Operations**
    - Focus: Kraus operators, quantum channels, decoherence

### Numerical Methods

11. **Johansson et al., "QuTiP: Quantum Toolbox in Python"**
    - Comput. Phys. Commun. 183, 1760 (2012)
    - https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cpc.2012.02.021
    - **Key**: Open source library for quantum dynamics simulation

12. **Schuchardt et al., "Quantum simulation methods for quantum chemistry"**
    - WIREs Comput. Mol. Sci. 11, e1493 (2021)
    - **Key**: Numerical methods for quantum dynamics

## Lecture Notes & Tutorials

13. **MIT OpenCourseWare: Quantum Physics III**
    - Course 8.06, Spring 2018
    - Focus: Spin dynamics, Larmor precession

14. **Stanford CS269Q: Quantum Computing**
    - Lecture notes on spin qubits
    - Focus: Quantum dot implementations
